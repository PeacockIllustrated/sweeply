FAQ

How quickly can I start working?

Once your identity and DBS check have been verified, you can start accepting bookings immediately.

Do I need my own transport?

No, but it can help you accept more bookings. Many clients are within walking or cycling distance.

How do payments work?

Clients pay through the Sweeply app. Funds are released to your Stripe or bank account once a job is completed.

Can I decline a job?

Yes. You control your availability and accept only the bookings that suit you.
