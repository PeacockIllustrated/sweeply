FAQs

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on your rates, hours and location. Many cleaners aim for £12–£20/hour. With the fee-free period you keep 100% for 28 days.

Do I choose my own clients?

Yes. You set availability and can accept or decline jobs. Clients rebook you directly once you’ve worked together.

Do I need a DBS check?

Not required to start, but recommended. A valid DBS unlocks higher visibility and “Trusted” badges.

Is there insurance?

We’ll offer optional cover at launch pricing. You can also use your own. Details provided during onboarding.
